About Us

Experts in learning innovation.

At Train in XR, we're always pushing the boundaries of what's possible with immersive technologies to help clients achieve their goals.

TXR is an immersive learning consultancy and advisory firm with our own team of VR and AR developers, 3D modellers, and learning & development professionals. We also work with third-party consultants who are ISO Standards experts. Established at the beginning of 2015, to date, TXR has developed over 500 VR and AR modules and applications that have trained more than a million learners in 12 countries.

All of us are here to help solve your workforce training challenges. To provide the best possible immersive solutions to our clients, we also have a global network of technology partners, VR and AR developers and specialist learning designers.
